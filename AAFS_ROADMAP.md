# AAFS Actionable Roadmap

## Phase I: Inception & Ethical Foundation (Months 1-2)
- [ ] Define the "Ethical Constitution" with interdisciplinary experts.
- [ ] Establish theological filters (BRI, Sharia, ESG).
- [ ] Design the conceptual architecture for the AI-Blockchain bridge.

## Phase II: Data Infrastructure & Revenue Integration (Months 3-5)
- [ ] Build the `aafs_revenue_ingest` pipeline.
- [ ] Secure real-time financial data feeds.
- [ ] Deploy the initial Blockchain ledger for tracking baseline operations.

## Phase III: Intelligence Development & Simulation (Months 6-10)
- [ ] Train the core AI models on historical data.
- [ ] Run "Ethical Stress Tests" in simulated market conditions.
- [ ] Develop the Human-in-the-Loop oversight dashboard.

## Phase IV: Pilot Launch & Collaborative Oversight (Months 11-14)
- [ ] Phased rollout to selected institutional partners.
- [ ] Real-time monitoring by human financial and ethical experts.
- [ ] Iterate based on performance and ethical alignment feedback.

## Phase V: Global Scaling & Self-Evolution (Months 15+)
- [ ] Full public deployment of the autonomous system.
- [ ] Integration of adaptive learning modules for continuous improvement.
- [ ] Expansion of theological filters to support a broader range of human values.
