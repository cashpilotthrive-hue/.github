# Chainlink: Strategy for the Global Standard in Onchain Finance

## Vision

A unified ecosystem of systems and a singular vision: to build Chainlink into the global standard for onchain finance.

## Overview

Chainlink is the industry-standard decentralized computing platform powering the verifiable web. This document outlines the strategy for establishing Chainlink as the foundational infrastructure layer for all onchain financial activity.

## Strategic Pillars

### 1. Universal Data Infrastructure

- Provide secure, reliable, and tamper-proof data feeds for all major asset classes (equities, commodities, FX, crypto)
- Expand Price Feed coverage to every significant financial market globally
- Deliver low-latency, high-frequency data required by next-generation DeFi protocols

### 2. Cross-Chain Interoperability

- Accelerate adoption of the Cross-Chain Interoperability Protocol (CCIP) as the universal messaging and token transfer standard
- Enable seamless movement of value and data across all major blockchains
- Partner with banks, exchanges, and financial institutions to bridge traditional finance and onchain markets

### 3. Proof of Reserve & Verifiable Transparency

- Expand Proof of Reserve services to provide real-time, cryptographically verified backing for tokenized assets
- Standardize onchain auditing practices for stablecoins, tokenized funds, and real-world assets (RWAs)
- Build trust with institutional participants through verifiable, auditable financial primitives

### 4. Institutional-Grade Automation

- Provide Chainlink Automation as the reliable execution layer for complex financial workflows
- Support conditional settlement, scheduled rebalancing, liquidation triggers, and compliance enforcement
- Meet the uptime and reliability requirements of regulated financial institutions

### 5. Tokenization of Real-World Assets

- Partner with asset managers, custodians, and market infrastructure providers to bring RWAs onchain
- Provide oracles and connectivity for tokenized bonds, equities, real estate, and private credit
- Enable 24/7 global settlement and programmable compliance for traditional financial instruments

## Ecosystem Development

### Developer Enablement

- Invest in documentation, SDKs, and tooling to make integration frictionless for developers
- Run grants programs and hackathons targeting DeFi, TradFi integration, and enterprise use cases
- Provide reference architectures for common onchain finance patterns

### Institutional Partnerships

- Engage with tier-1 banks, asset managers, and fintech companies to pilot and scale onchain finance solutions
- Work with regulators and standards bodies to establish best practices for oracle usage in regulated environments
- Build joint go-to-market programs with blockchain networks, custody providers, and compliance vendors

### Standards Adoption

- Actively contribute to industry standards (ISO, IOSCO, BIS) for blockchain-based financial systems
- Publish open specifications for data quality, oracle behavior, and cross-chain communication
- Foster an open ecosystem where Chainlink's standards are adopted across multiple platforms

## Success Metrics

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Total Value Secured (TVS) | Top global oracle network |
| Number of supported blockchains | All major EVM and non-EVM chains |
| Institutional integrations | Tier-1 banks and asset managers |
| RWA assets supported | Bonds, equities, real estate, commodities |
| CCIP transaction volume | Industry-leading cross-chain volume |

## Roadmap Phases

### Phase 1 — Foundation (Current)
- Solidify core oracle infrastructure and Data Feeds coverage
- Launch CCIP on all major blockchains
- Establish Proof of Reserve for key stablecoin and RWA issuers

### Phase 2 — Expansion
- Deepen institutional partnerships and TradFi integrations
- Scale tokenized asset support through standardized oracle connectivity
- Grow the developer ecosystem with improved tooling and grants

### Phase 3 — Global Standard
- Achieve adoption as the default oracle and interoperability layer across the financial system
- Integrate with central bank digital currency (CBDC) infrastructure
- Establish Chainlink as the trust layer for a fully interconnected onchain financial system

## Conclusion

By combining robust decentralized infrastructure, deep institutional partnerships, and an open standards approach, Chainlink is positioned to become the global standard for onchain finance — connecting every system, asset class, and financial institution to a single, verifiable, and permissionless financial fabric.
